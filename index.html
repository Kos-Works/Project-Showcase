Final Year University Project · 6 DoF Robot and Simulation

Robotics · Simulation · Mechatronics

  • Built and simulated a cable actuated soft surgical manipulator with six degrees of freedom.
  • Integrated friction aware modelling and inverse kinematics into a MATLAB and Arduino pipeline that maps endpoint targets to cable lengths and servo commands.
  • Physical prototyping validated geometry but exposed high internal friction and stick slip, which limited curvature and overloaded servos and revealed a simulation to hardware gap.
  • Fabrication established a repeatable workflow using resin moulds, ejector pins and Ecoflex.
MATLAB Arduino Inverse kinematics

Night Vision System

Systems engineering · CAD · Electronics

  • Designed and manufactured a custom housing using SolidWorks and Cura, optimised for compactness and durability.
  • Wired and soldered multiple subsystems including drone cameras, lenses, screens and IR floodlights to achieve clear imaging in low light conditions.
  • Added connectivity to an FPV drone to demonstrate systems integration and forward looking application of technology.
  • Overcame challenges in power management, alignment of optics and ensuring stable electronic connections.
  • Enhanced soldering and wiring skills as well as troubleshooting under real constraints.
  • Produced a working prototype capable of real time, low latency night vision to validate design choices and technical execution.
